Hi, I'm Nico  👋🏼

Let's get the obvious out of the way: I'm not a developer. I don't write production code, I can't review your PR, and I'm not going to open a meaningful issue on your repo. What I am is someone who has spent over a decade trying to understand what's broken about the way technical talent gets found, evaluated, and placed — and who believes the best way to do that is to show up where the talent actually "lives". So here I am.

**Why GitHub?**  🤔

LinkedIn profiles and resumes flatten — they reduce complex, curious, creative people into a list of bullet points and keywords that rarely tell the real story. A GitHub profile, on the other hand? That's a body of work. That's commitment, curiosity, and craft made visible. I'm here to learn how to read that story better.

**What I Actually Do**  🤝🏼

I'm a Recruiter with a background in Cultural Anthropology and Conflict Resolution — which sounds like an odd combination until you realize that recruiting, at its best, is just applied anthropology. You're studying communities, understanding what motivates people, and finding where interests align. I'm shifting my focus to specialize in technical (AI/ML) recruiting, and I'm currently co-building an AI-powered sourcing and outreach tool with a 30-year DevOps and AI veteran — because I believe the recruiting industry is overdue for a serious rethink. Juicebox beat me to the punch, but I'm still tinkering to learn and grow in this space. 

**What I'm Looking For**  ☘️

Honestly? Connections and conversations. If you're a software engineer, AI/ML practitioner, or anything in between — and you're open to hearing about interesting opportunities — I'd genuinely love to connect. I promise I'm not going to spam you with a generic "I came across your profile and thought you'd be a great fit" message. That's not how I operate. I believe in the matchmaking model: understanding what you actually want, what you're actually good at, and finding the overlap with something worth your time.

**What I'm Not**  🙅🏼‍♂️

A developer cosplaying as one to seem relatable, someone who will waste your time, or simply interested in checking boxes

**Let's Connect**  🤙🏼

If you want to talk — whether it's about an opportunity, the state of AI recruiting, or just to tell me what recruiters consistently get wrong about engineers — my door is open.

I'm a big fan of big brains, here to find the humans behind the commits, learn, and grow.  🙌🏼
